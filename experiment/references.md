

1. **Domingo-Ferrer, J.** (2002). *Inference Control in Statistical Databases*. Springer.  
   Core reference for inference attacks and control mechanisms in statistical databases.

2. **Denning, D. E. R.** (1982). *Cryptography and Data Security*. Addison-Wesley.  
   Foundational work covering statistical database security and information leakage.

3. **Aggarwal, C. C., & Yu, P. S. (Eds.).** (2008). *Privacy-Preserving Data Mining: Models and Algorithms*. Springer.  
   Discusses privacy models and techniques to prevent data leakage.

4. **[Differential Privacy — Protecting Individual Information in Aggregate Queries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Differential_privacy)**  
   Explains modern privacy-preserving techniques for preventing inference attacks.

5. **[k-Anonymity — Model for Protecting Privacy in Data Releases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K-anonymity)**  
   Describes a key technique for protecting individual data in statistical databases.
